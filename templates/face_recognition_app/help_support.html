Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new student?

1. Click on "Students" in the sidebar
2. Click "Add New Student" button
3. Fill in the student details
4. Click "Save Student"

How do I update student information?

1. Go to the Students list
2. Click the edit (pencil) icon next to the student
3. Update the required information
4. Click "Save Student"

How do I take student photos?

1. Go to student details page
2. Click "Take Photos" button
3. Ensure good lighting and clear face visibility
4. Follow the on-screen instructions

Why isn't face recognition working?

1. Ensure the model is trained
2. Check if student photos are added
3. Verify proper lighting conditions
4. Make sure the camera is working

How do I view attendance records?

1. Click "Attendance" in the sidebar
2. Use filters to find specific records
3. Export to CSV if needed

Can I manually add attendance?

Yes! Click "Add Manual Entry" in the attendance page and fill in the details.

Troubleshooting Guide
Common Issues
  • Camera not working - Check browser permissions
  • Face not detected - Ensure proper lighting
  • Recognition fails - Retrain the model
  • System slow - Clear browser cache
Best Practices
  • Use well-lit environments for face recognition
  • Take multiple photos per student
  • Regularly backup attendance data
  • Keep the system updated
